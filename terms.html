5. Permissions and Their Purpose

The App requests the following Android permissions. Granting them is required for the relevant features to function:

  • Precise and background location: Required for geofence-based Routines and auto prayer time calculation.
  • Read contacts: Required for the Emergency Bypass Contacts feature.
  • Read phone state / call log: Required to detect calls from bypass contacts and missed calls for auto-SMS.
  • Send SMS / receive SMS: Required for the Auto SMS Reply feature.
  • Read calendar: Required for calendar event-based Routines.
  • Notification policy access (Do Not Disturb): Required to set DND mode.
  • Schedule exact alarms: Required for time-precise Routine scheduling.
  • Receive boot completed: Required to restart active Routines after device reboot.
  • Foreground service: Required to keep active Routines running reliably in the background.
  • Battery optimisation exemption: Requested (not required) to prevent Android from killing background services prematurely.

You may revoke any permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Muffle → Permissions. Revoking a permission will disable the corresponding feature.
